Our Mission

Located in the bustling metropolitan Lafourche, Louisiane we aim to not only bring to you impressive delicacies of French cuisine, but the indominatable excellency of French military might.

We offer authentic French dining in a elegant setting complete with complementary croissants for any order, and many wine choices to round out a good meal. Additionally, enjoy watching live battles from the Napoleonic era, complete with field cannons! On weekends, witness special spectacles such as officer duels and educational experiences such as the changing of the guard and the process of loading a cannon. Or on Sunday, witness duels for the centuries as anachronistic foes square off toe to toe such as Vercingetorix and Napoleon himself!

In lieu of visiting Wellington's Bar n Brawl in the northern Colonies, or Don Pedro Hernandez Cortez de la Cruz' Cantina and Cannons in the western territories, why not treat yourself to a truly gentlemanly and cultured dining experience in a land named after our good king, Louis! (Don't be a smart alek and ask which, a true monsieur knows!)
